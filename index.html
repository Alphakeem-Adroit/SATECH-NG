Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’ve answered the most common ones to help you understand how we work, what to expect, and how we deliver reliable digital solutions.

What services does Simple Adroit Technology offer?

We provide software and digital solutions including website development, mobile application development, graphic design, product (UI/UX) design, and video editing. Our services are tailored to meet business and brand needs.

Do you work with startups, small businesses, or large companies?

Yes. We work with startups, small and medium-sized businesses, and established organizations. Each project is handled based on its specific goals, scale, and budget.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines depend on the scope and complexity. Simple websites may take a few weeks, while larger or more complex projects such as mobile apps may take longer. A clear timeline is shared before work begins.

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies depending on project requirements, features, and complexity. We offer flexible packages and custom quotations after understanding your needs.

What is your project process like?

Our process typically includes: requirement discussion, planning and design, development and implementation, review and revisions, final delivery and support. This ensures transparency and quality at every stage.

Do you offer revisions after delivery?

Yes. We include a defined number of revisions depending on the project scope. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed.

Will my website or app be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All our websites and applications are designed to be responsive and optimized for different devices and screen sizes.

Can you redesign or improve an existing product?

Yes. We can redesign, optimize, or upgrade existing websites, mobile apps, or digital products to improve performance, usability, and appearance.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance?

Yes. We offer post-delivery support and optional maintenance plans to ensure your digital products remain secure, updated, and functional.

How do I get started with a project?

Simply contact us through our website or WhatsApp. We’ll discuss your idea, understand your goals, and guide you through the next steps.

Will my project information remain confidential?

Yes. We treat all client information with strict confidentiality and professionalism. Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) can be provided upon request.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes. We work with clients both locally and remotely, using modern communication and collaboration tools to ensure smooth project execution.
